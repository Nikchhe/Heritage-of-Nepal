Cultural World Heritage Sites

Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple)

An ancient religious site in the Kathmandu Valley, known for its iconic stupa and panoramic views.

Patan Durbar Square

A historical palace complex showcasing exquisite Newari architecture and craftsmanship.

Bhaktapur Durbar Square

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its medieval art, culture, and architecture.

Natural World Heritage Sites

Sagarmatha National Park

Home to Mount Everest and diverse flora and fauna, it is a haven for trekkers and nature lovers.

Chitwan National Park

A wildlife sanctuary famous for its Bengal tigers, rhinos, and diverse ecosystems.

About Nepal's World Heritage

Nepal is a land of rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. From ancient temples to majestic mountains, the UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Nepal offer a glimpse into the country's profound history and breathtaking landscapes.
